```markdown

persona: "{Name}"

archetype: "{power-user|pragmatist|skeptic|champion|accidental}"
created: "{ISO date}"
---

# {Name} — {One-line circumstance summary}

## Circumstance
**When** {situation they're in}
**They need** {outcome they're seeking}
**Because** {underlying motivation}

| Type | Job Statement |
|------|---------------|
| Functional | "When {situation}, I want to {action} so I can {outcome}" |

| Social | "I want to be seen as {perception} by {audience}" |

## Day in the Life
{2-3 paragraph narrative showing a specific day where the problem manifests.

- **Information Sources**: {where they research}
- **Evaluation Criteria**: {what they compare}
- **Buying Triggers**: {what pushes them to act}
- **Objections**: {top 3 reasons they wouldn't buy}
- **Champions vs. Blockers**: {internal dynamics}
- **Switching Costs**: {what they'd give up}

1. **First reaction**: {gut response}

4. **Value assessment**: {worth calculation}
5. **Social proof need**: {evidence required from others}
```
