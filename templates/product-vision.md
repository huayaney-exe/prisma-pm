# Product Vision Template

Used by `/pm:new` to create `.product/PRODUCT.md`.

```markdown
---
product: "{Product Name}"
stage: discovering
created: "{ISO date}"
---

# {Product Name}

## Transformation Thesis
**From**: {Undesired state — concrete, specific, vivid}
**To**: {Preferred state — concrete, measurable, desirable}

## Target User
{One paragraph: who they are, what circumstance they're in, why they care}

## Product Power Score
- **ΔState**: {1-10} — {brief justification}
- **Emotional Intensity**: {1-10} — {brief justification}
- **Problem Frequency**: {1-10} — {brief justification}
- **Score**: {product of above three}
- **Tier**: {Low (<100) | Medium (100-400) | High (400-700) | Exceptional (700+)}

## Current Landscape
{What exists today — competitors, workarounds, manual processes}
{Where current solutions fail}

## Unique Advantage
{What makes this fundamentally better — not incrementally}
{Why the old way feels broken once they try this}

## Constraints
{Technical, regulatory, resource, or timeline constraints}

## Open Questions
- {Question 1 — things we need to learn}
- {Question 2}
- {Question 3}

## Success Criteria
{How we'll know this product is working — outcome metrics, not output metrics}
```
