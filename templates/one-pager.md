# One-Pager Template

Used by `/pm:define --format one-pager` for stakeholder communication.

```markdown
# {Feature Name} — One Pager

**Author**: {name} | **Date**: {date} | **Status**: {draft | review | approved}

## The Problem
{2-3 sentences: who has the problem, what the problem is, how often it happens.
Make it vivid — stakeholders should feel the pain.}

## The Solution
{2-3 sentences: what we're building and how it solves the problem.
Focus on the transformation, not the feature list.}

## Why Now
{1-2 sentences: what makes this urgent. Market timing, competitive pressure,
user demand, strategic alignment.}

## Success Metrics
- {Metric 1}: {baseline} → {target}
- {Metric 2}: {baseline} → {target}
- {Metric 3}: {baseline} → {target}

## Product Power
**Score**: {X} ({tier})
- ΔState: {X}/10 | Intensity: {Y}/10 | Frequency: {Z}/10

## Timeline
{One line: "MVP in X weeks, full feature in Y weeks"}

## Resources
{One line: "{N} engineers, {N} designers for {duration}"}

## Risks
- {Risk 1 and mitigation}
- {Risk 2 and mitigation}

## What We're NOT Doing
- {Explicit exclusion 1}
- {Explicit exclusion 2}
```
